Shore-to-Core-to-Shore Tax Increment Financing (TIF) District


Plans to Revitalize Downtown Cleveland

What is Shore-to-Core-to-Shore?

Shore-to-Core-to-Shore is an initiative in the City of Cleveland, Ohio to redevelop and transform downtown Cleveland. The plan aims to generate between 3.5 and 7.5 billion dollars worth of revenue through a TIF District to revitalize the lakefront, the downtown core, and the riverfront.
