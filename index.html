Plans to Revitalize Downtown Cleveland

What is Shore-to-Core-to-Shore?

Shore-to-Core-to-Shore is an initiative in the City of Cleveland, Ohio to redevelop and transform downtown Cleveland. The plan aims to generate between 3.5 and 7.5 billion dollars worth of revenue through a Tax Increment Financing District to revitalize the lakefront, the downtown core, and the riverfront.

What is the Shore-to-Core-to-Shore Tax Increment Financing District?

The Shore-to-Core-to-Shore Tax Increment Financing district, or TIF, is a designated zone where a portion of new property tax revenue generated by an increase in property values are redirected to a separate fund. The dollars in the fund are then used for public investments, and in this case, the City of Cleveland can spend funds throughout the city, but will focuse on substantial investments along the lakefront and riverfront in the downtown core.
